About Us

J-Petroleum is a sister company to Ujenzi, specializing in the petroleum industry. Our core activities include:

  • Exploration and drilling of oil wells
  • Refining and processing of crude oil
  • Distribution and marketing of petroleum products
  • Research and development in the energy sector
  • Environmental sustainability initiatives

Why Choose J-Petroleum

J-Petroleum is dedicated to delivering reliable and sustainable solutions in the petroleum industry. We are committed to excellence and provide the following reasons to choose us:

  • 01Exploration and Drilling

    J-Petroleum excels in the exploration and drilling of oil wells, ensuring a secure and efficient energy source for various applications.

  • 02Refining and Processing

    Our state-of-the-art facilities specialize in the refining and processing of crude oil, delivering high-quality petroleum products to meet diverse market demands.

  • 03Distribution and Marketing

    J-Petroleum ensures efficient distribution and marketing of petroleum products, contributing to the global energy supply chain.
